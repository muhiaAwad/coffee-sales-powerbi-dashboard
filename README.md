#  Coffee Sales Dashboard - Power BI

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes coffee shop sales data using Power BI to understand business performance, customer behavior, and product trends across the year.

The dashboard was designed to provide a clear executive summary along with visual insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze total sales performance
- Identify best and worst performing months
- Determine top-selling products
- Understand customer payment behavior
- Calculate key business metrics

---

##  Key Metrics
- **Total Sales:** 115K
- **Total Orders:** 4K
- **Average Order Value:** 32

---

##  Insights & Key Findings

###  Sales Trend Analysis
- Sales fluctuated throughout the year.
- March recorded the highest sales, indicating a seasonal peak.
- April showed a noticeable drop in revenue.
- October demonstrated another strong performance.
- Sales patterns suggest possible seasonal customer behavior.

###  Product Performance
- **Latte** is the best-selling product and generates the highest revenue.
- **Americano with Milk** and **Cappuccino** are strong contributors.
- **Espresso** is the lowest-performing product in terms of revenue.
- Revenue is concentrated in a few high-demand products.

###  Payment Behavior
- 97% of total sales are made via **card payments**.
- Only around 3% of sales are paid in cash.
- This indicates strong customer preference for digital payments.
- Reliable electronic payment systems are critical for operations.

###  Business Implications
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing products like Latte.
- Investigate the sales drop in April to improve future performance.
- Use seasonal peaks (e.g., March, October) for targeted promotions.
- Maintain and optimize card payment infrastructure.
